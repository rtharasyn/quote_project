Family
Put your family in the center. They are the central hub for love, support, friendship, comfort and motivation. Work hard to provide for them as they have provided for you. Give them everything you have and never take them for granted.
Career
Follow your dreams confidently and create the world in which you want to live. Exemplify your beliefs, and keep your hands dirty. Take pride in your work and elevate those around you. Always remember; nobody owes you anything and nothing is beneath you.
Health
Be prepared for the sport of life. Build mental and physical strength and stamina. Create sustainable habits focused on mind and body. Deliver in the moment but not at the detriment of the future, avoiding all shortcuts.
Lifestyle
Create a sustainable, balanced life with quality and purpose. Live well and enjoy good fortune while preparing for a brighter tomorrow. Pay attention to the details and eliminate waste.
Growth
Grow insatiably. Work continuously to improve and expand yourself. Take responsibility for your short comings and close gaps before they become critical inhibitors.
